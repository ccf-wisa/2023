Paper Submission

All papers must be submitted through the CMT system, via the following link.

https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/WISA2023

All submissions must be written in English and conform to the Springer LNCS> proceedings format with the following page limits: 12 pages for regular papers and 8 pages for short papers. Submitted papers will undergo a double-blind review process, coordinated by the Program Committee. To ensure anonymity of authorship, authors must ensure that authors' names, affiliations, funding, and any other identifying information of authorship do not appear on the title page or elsewhere in the paper.

Authors should submit papers reporting original work that are currently not under review or published elsewhere. Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings, which will be published as a volume of Springer's Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series.
