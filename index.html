The 20th CCF Conference on
Web Information Systems and Applications
(WISA 2023)

WISA 2023 is organized by the CCF Technical Committee on Information Systems (CCF TCIS) and Sichuan University. WISA 2023 provides a premium forum for researchers, professionals, practitioners and officers closely related to information systems and applications, which enables them to discuss the theme about the future intelligent information systems with big data, focuses on difficult critical problems, and fosters innovative technology for new application areas of information systems.

The theme of WISA 2023 is Digital Intelligence and Information Systems, focuses on digital intelligence, smart information systems, digital transformation, security of information systems, etc., and emphasizes the key issues in metaverse, knowledge graphs, blockchains, etc.

The program of WISA 2023 features several key note talks by renowned experts in the important areas of information systems, to share their cutting edge technology and views about the industrial hotspots. The other events include topic-specific forums, industrial forums, TCIS salon, and PhD forum.

The topics of interest include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Information system architecture
  • Data fusion of information systems
  • Data mining in information systems
  • Information storage in information systems
  • Query processing in information systems
  • Information retrieval in information systems
  • Security of information systems
  • Visualization and information systems
  • Big data and information systems
  • Knowledge graph and information systems
  • Blockchain and information systems
  • Machine learning and information systems
  • Intelligent decision and formation systems
  • Recommender systems and information systems
  • NLP in information systems
  • Information systems in post-pandemic
